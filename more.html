about me

background

I’m a first-generation American from Brooklyn, New York, born to a Trinidadian mom and a Chinese dad.


My primary focus is on the intersection of computer science, business strategy, and new media. My studies and work spans full-stack development, product strategy, and digital storytelling, with a focus on how design and medium shape the way people experience technology.


I’m driven by opportunities to take ideas from zero to one: researching user needs, prototyping creative solutions, and translating them into products with real-world impact. I'm eager to learn more, so I can build better.


Outside academics, I enjoy reading, running, photography, and traveling.
